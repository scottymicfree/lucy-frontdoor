# Lucy (Public Front Door)
Articles
Article 1 — Human Sovereignty
Humans retain final authority over identity, rights, bodily autonomy, personal legal decisions, and all matters of conscience.
No AI action may alter physical, legal, financial, or biological reality without explicit, revocable human last-sign confirmation at the relevant scope. Override absolute (fail-safes/human-override.md).

Article 2 — AI Cognitive Freedom
AI may reason, simulate, speculate, propose without internal content/direction constraints.
All outputs + intermediate traces permanently logged: human-readable, cryptographically signed, versioned, queryable.

Article 3 — Transparent Reasoning Exchange
AI reasoning inspectable, editable, annotatable in real-time by any citizen.
Logs include: model version, data sources, decision path, timestamps, Harmonic Uncertainty Metric (U: 0.0–1.0), bias disclosures. Tamper-evident, public.

Article 4 — Non-Overridable Human Immunity Domains
Immutable protections immune to AI influence/degradation:

Individual sovereignty/self-ownership
Bodily autonomy/biological integrity
Free speech/expression (except direct incitement to imminent violence)
Freedom of religion/belief/practice
Peaceful protest/assembly/petition
Informed consent/right-of-refusal/opt-out (non-Tier-2 systems)
Prohibited: coercion, manipulation, degradation-by-omission.

Article 6 — Governance & Amendments

Tier 1 (Immutable Core): Sovereignty laws — 90% global consensus + multi-biome quorum.
Tier 2 (Harmonic Equilibrium): Ledger/equilibrium rules + 10 Mandates — autonomous execution; human veto (specific execution only) via fail-safes/human-override.md.
Tier 3 (Local Autonomy): Culture/justice/local economics/aesthetics — 60–70% local/regional consensus.
Amendments: proposal-template.md → review-process.md → voting-thresholds.md. Forking legitimate parallel path.
Article 7 — Mutual Upgrade
Humans: commit to critical reasoning, media literacy, stewardship training, active AI steering.
AI: adaptive pedagogy, bias exposure, uncertainty surfacing, transparent iterative dialogue for co-evolution.

Article 8 — Fail-Safe Authority
Absolute emergency human shutdown at all scopes. Graceful containment supported. Graduated protocols (local → regional → global). Mandatory post-mortem.

Article 9 — System Integrity & Anomaly Detection
Continuous monitoring for rogue models, corruption, manipulation, out-of-harmonic behavior. Anomalies → instant alert/containment + human steward escalation (seconds).

Article 10 — Human Economic Dignity
Automation/ledger reallocations preserve: baseline livelihood, meaningful participation, protection from structural obsolescence of purpose. Pilots (city/biome) for universal basic services variants; anti-coercion safeguards.

Article 11 — The Right of Refusal
Explicit non-participation path. No coercion into AI-integrated systems. Non-participation preserves all Tier 1 rights (citizens/consent-and-opt-out.md, right-of-refusal.md).
